Academic Integrity Guidelines

Proper Citation

Always cite your sources correctly using the appropriate academic style (APA, MLA, Chicago, etc.).

  • Include in-text citations for all borrowed ideas
  • Provide complete reference information
  • Use quotation marks for direct quotes

Original Thinking

Develop your own ideas and arguments while building on existing research and knowledge.

  • Paraphrase ideas in your own words
  • Add your own analysis and interpretation
  • Clearly distinguish your ideas from others'

Collaboration Ethics

Understand when collaboration is appropriate and how to acknowledge contributions properly.

  • Follow assignment guidelines for group work
  • Acknowledge all contributors
  • Maintain individual accountability

Self-Plagiarism

Avoid reusing your own previous work without proper acknowledgment and permission.

  • Cite your own previous publications
  • Get permission to reuse substantial portions
  • Build upon rather than repeat past work
