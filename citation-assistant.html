Supported Citation Formats

APA Style

American Psychological Association format, widely used in psychology, education, and social sciences.

Example:
Smith, J. A. (2023). Research methods in psychology. Academic Press.

MLA Style

Modern Language Association format, commonly used in literature, arts, and humanities.

Example:
Smith, John A. Research Methods in Psychology. Academic Press, 2023.

Chicago Style

Chicago Manual of Style, used in history, literature, and the arts with two citation systems.

Example:
Smith, John A. Research Methods in Psychology. Chicago: Academic Press, 2023.

IEEE Style

Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers format, standard in engineering and technology.

Example:
J. A. Smith, Research Methods in Psychology. Academic Press, 2023.

Citation Quick Guide

Pro Tips

  • Always double-check author names and publication dates
  • Use DOIs when available for journal articles
  • Include page numbers for direct quotes
  • Keep track of access dates for online sources
  • Maintain consistency throughout your document

Common Mistakes

  • Incorrect punctuation and capitalization
  • Missing publication information
  • Inconsistent date formats
  • Wrong italicization of titles
  • Incomplete author information
